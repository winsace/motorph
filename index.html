Dev Notes:
Greetings Team,

As we embark on the development journey of the MotorPH Payroll System, it's imperative to keep our focus aligned with the project objectives and requirements. Our primary goal is to construct a user-friendly portal that caters to the diverse needs of both employees and HR personnel within the MotorPH organization.

For the employee-facing functionalities, we aim to provide a seamless experience where individuals can effortlessly access their profiles, submit leave requests, create tickets for any concerns, conveniently record their attendance through the timesheet punch-in/out feature, and securely view their payslips. These functionalities should be intuitive and responsive, ensuring ease of use for all employees.

On the administrative side, HR personnel will be empowered with comprehensive CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionalities for efficient employee management. This includes tasks such as adding new employees, updating existing records, managing leave requests, and overseeing attendance records. Security measures must be robust to safeguard sensitive employee data and ensure compliance with privacy regulations.

The Payroll module will be a cornerstone of our system, automating the complex task of calculating payslips using specialized tools and generating accurate payment records for all employees. Reliability and precision are paramount in this aspect, as any inaccuracies could lead to significant repercussions.

Additionally, the IT team's responsibilities encompass the creation and maintenance of user accounts, password resets, account disabling, and the overall upkeep of the portal to guarantee its seamless operation round the clock. Continuous monitoring and proactive maintenance are crucial to prevent any disruptions in service.

Let's maintain clear communication channels, adhere to best coding practices, and prioritize collaboration to deliver a high-quality, robust solution that meets the expectations of MotorPH. Regular updates and feedback sessions will be instrumental in ensuring that we stay on track and address any challenges promptly.

Together, let's embark on this journey with diligence, creativity, and a shared commitment to excellence.

Best regards,
